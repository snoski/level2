Serendipity

Perhaps the start of a new chapter?

I feel that it is serendipity that I am sitting here working on this application to Holberton School at this moment.

What is serendipity?

Oxford defines serendipity as "the occurrence and development of events by chance in a happy or beneficial way."

An unlikely but fortunate occurence

Just feeling like I needed a change to shake things up, I moved to Tulsa on July 9, 2021 and moved in with a roommate I had never met before. He seemed like a nice enough fellow, but I decided to join him at the Mercury Lounge to get to know him a little better.

During our discussion, the topic of coincidences emerged. I mentioned that a book called the Celestine Prophecy has had a significant meaningful impact on my life. I explained that the book had a very new-agey explanation for why we should pay attention to coincidences. While I didn't buy into the woo-woo explanation, I did find the book's central idea of paying attention to coincides important.

I continued that if we noticed it AS a coincidence then it must have an importance to us. It caught our attention for SOME reason. The reason may not be known to our consciousness, but it is very likely known to our subconscious.

Almost immediately as I had completed that thought a couple walked up and begin speaking to my roommate asking about his Tulsa Remote t-shirt. That was the first coincidence.

My roommate is not a part of Tulsa Remote, but he's friends with quite a few who are. Yet he happened to be wearing that t-shirt that night, and it turns out the man works for Tulsa Remote.

We began chatting with the couple. While my roommate was discussing Tulsa Remote, I asked the woman what she does for a living. She told me that she works for a software engineering school here in Tulsa called Holberton. That was the second coincidence.

My ears immediately perked up, and I said, "Tell me more!" You see, I've been programming as a solo amateur for around 13 years. I've even incorporated it into my career, but I've always entertained the idea of making the pivot to coding full-time.

As she described the program, I couldn't believe the fact that these coincidences happened almost immediately after my roommate and I had been discussing the importance of paying attention to coincidences. I definitely felt like the universe (or perhaps my own subconscious) was telling me something.

Long story short, after an email or two and a phone conversation, I began my application. I am so excited to see where this path will lead!
